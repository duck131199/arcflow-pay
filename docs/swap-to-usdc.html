Swap to USDC legacy note

This page is kept so older links do not break. Product-facing UI should say Convert to USDC, not Swap to USDC.

Current wording

Convert to USDC

Convert to USDC is the current alpha helper inside Pay Invoice. It helps a payer convert supported Arc Testnet assets into Arc USDC before submitting an invoice payment.

  • Convert does not automatically pay the invoice.
  • The payer still reviews and submits the Arc USDC payment from Pay Invoice.
  • Use the Move and Convert USDC guide for the current flow.

Product boundary

Payment remains separate

Arqis should not auto-spend, auto-swap, bridge, or route funds without explicit payer approval. Preparing Arc USDC and paying an invoice are separate steps in the current MVP.

Technical code may still use swap-related names internally, but user-facing copy should prefer Convert to USDC.

Simple rule: Convert prepares Arc USDC. Move brings USDC to Arc. Pay Invoice submits the invoice payment.
