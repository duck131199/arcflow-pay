Why it matters

Enough value, wrong assets

Payers may have enough total value to pay an invoice, but spread across USDC, BNB, ETH, BTC, and tiny dust balances.

Product flow

From invoice to settlement

Seller creates invoice
Arqis generates QR/payment link
Payer scans or opens checkout
Payer chooses asset/source
Payment settles on Arc
Seller tracks payment in Seller Console
MVP principle

Small first

The current MVP validates invoice payment UX before adding API, webhooks, advanced analytics, or agent payments.

Payer control

No hidden routing

Arqis should not automatically spend, swap, bridge, or route funds without explicit payer confirmation.
