# Arqis Brand Notes

Arqis is a stablecoin-native invoice inbox and seller console prototype for Arc Testnet.

## Short description

USDC invoice payments on Arc Testnet, with a seller-first console and future-ready routing direction.

## Current positioning

- Seller-first stablecoin invoice payments.
- USDC settlement first.
- Future-ready for payer-side swap/bridge routing.
- Testnet prototype; not audited; no mainnet funds.
