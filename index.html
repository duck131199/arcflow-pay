Arqis

Simple USDC invoice payments on Arc for individuals and small businesses: create an invoice, let the payer pay with Arc Testnet USDC, track settlement, and help testers get setup.

Testnet MVP: Current checkout supports USDC on Arc Testnet. Invoice links, QR checkout, and payment routing are planned. Wallet QR is for direct transfers only.
