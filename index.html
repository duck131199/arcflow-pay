Arqis

Simple USDC invoice payments on Arc for individuals and small businesses: create an invoice, let the payer pay with Arc Testnet USDC, track settlement, and help testers get setup.

Testnet MVP: Current checkout supports USDC on Arc Testnet through the payer invoice inbox. Wallet QR is for direct transfers only.

Seller creates invoice

Keep this step simple. The seller only enters invoice details.

Enter a registered Arqis payer name.
Payer wallet resolves automatically from the Arqis name.
Amount must be 1-50,000 USDC.
Memo is required and limited to 99 characters.
