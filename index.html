ArcFlow Pay

Simple USDC invoice payments on Arc for individuals and small businesses: create an invoice, let the payer choose an asset, track settlement, and help testers get Arc Testnet USDC.

Seller creates invoice

Keep this step simple. The seller only enters invoice details.

Seller settlement wallet is configured once in Seller Settings, not per invoice.
For this prototype, wallet addresses, hashes, invoices, and balances are demo data.

Payment link / QR

Create an invoice to generate a desktop payment link and mobile invoice QR.
Flow
Seller creates invoice QR/link → Buyer scans or opens checkout → Buyer pays from any supported source → Seller receives USDC settlement on Arc.
